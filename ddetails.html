The Maldives.

The Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, made up of over 1,000 coral islands grouped into 26 atolls. It's famous for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, white-sand beaches, luxurious overwater bungalows, and vibrant marine life. A popular destination for honeymooners and luxury travelers, the Maldives offers world-class diving, snorkeling, and serene island escapes.

6-Day Maldives Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

Arrive in Malé, transfer to the resort by speedboat or seaplane, relax on the beach, and enjoy a sunset dinner.

Day 2: Snorkeling

Morning snorkeling tour, paddleboarding or spa session, and an optional sunset dolphin cruise.

Day 3: Water Sports

Try jet skiing or kayaking, enjoy a sandbank picnic, and end the day with a beach BBQ dinner.

Day 4: Culture

Visit a nearby local island, learn about Maldivian traditions, and relax with a romantic dinner.

Day 5: Departure

Take a sunrise walk, have breakfast with a view, and transfer back to Malé for your flight home.

Day 6: Luxury Chill

Spend your final full day unwinding by the lagoon, enjoying floating breakfast, or lounging in an overwater hammock.

Santorini,Greece.

Santorini is a stunning island in the Aegean Sea, famous for its whitewashed buildings with blue domes, dramatic cliffs, and unforgettable sunsets. Known as one of the most romantic destinations in the world, Santorini offers charming villages, volcanic beaches, and breathtaking views from every angle. Whether you're exploring ancient ruins, enjoying local wine, or relaxing on a cliffside terrace, Santorini promises a truly magical escape.

6-Day Santorini Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

Arrival in Santorini, check-in, and evening walk through Fira. Enjoy dinner overlooking the caldera.

Day 2: History

Visit the ancient ruins of Akrotiri, then relax at Red Beach. End with a sunset wine tasting.

Day 3: Beach

Spend the day at Perissa or Kamari Beach. Swim, relax, or enjoy watersports.

Day 4: Cruise

Join a boat tour to the volcano and hot springs. Optional sunset cruise with dinner onboard.

Day 5: Oia

Explore Oia’s charming streets and shops. Watch the famous sunset from the castle ruins.

Day 6: Farewell

Relax in a quiet café or stroll through Megalochori. Departure from Santorini.
